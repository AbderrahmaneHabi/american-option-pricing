# Institutional Derivatives Underwriting Desk

A full-stack quantitative application that prices American put options using the Longstaff-Schwartz Least Squares Monte Carlo (LSM) method. 

## Features
* **Manual Parameter Estimation:** Derives moments (volatility, drift) from first principles using historical data.
* **Risk-Neutral GBM Simulation:** Monte Carlo framework tracking 10,000 independent asset paths.
* **American Put Pricing:** Polynomial regression backwards induction solving for early exercise premiums.
* **Backtesting Engine:** Ingests live 2025 market data to evaluate realistic payoffs and client behavioral triggers.
* **Portfolio Matrix:** Multi-asset ledger tracking aggregate PnL for both the clearinghouse and the buyer.
